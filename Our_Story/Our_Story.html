about the company

Narayan Holidays

Narayan Holidays is a leading and well-established Destination Management Company (DMC) in Uttarakhand, proudly headquartered in Haridwar. With over five successful years of experience, we have been consistently serving our valued guests with a strong foundation of trust, reliability, and service excellence.
Over the years, Narayan Holidays has grown steadily with a clear and unwavering objective complete customer satisfaction. We strongly believe that travel is not just about destinations, but about experiences. Therefore, we focus on crafting unforgettable journeys rather than simply selling tour packages. Each itinerary is thoughtfully designed to ensure comfort, safety, and memorable experiences that our guests cherish long after their journey ends.

  • Rated 4.9 ★ Based on 502 Reviews

Our guest-first philosophy, sincerity, and professional commitment distinguish us in the travel industry and have earned us the confidence of travelers and business partners alike. Backed by one of the largest and most reliable networks of hotels, transportation services, and logistics partners across Uttarakhand, we are able to deliver seamless, efficient, and high-quality travel solutions.

In recent years, Narayan Holidays has earned a strong reputation for organizing the Char Dham Yatra in a truly luxurious and well-coordinated manner, including exclusive helicopter services, offering pilgrims a spiritually fulfilling yet comfortable and time-efficient experience.

With deep-rooted local expertise and a passion for excellence, Narayan Holidays continues to be a trusted name for exploring the authentic essence of Uttarakhand.

Our Motive

The motive of Narayan Holidays is to go beyond conventional travel services and provide every guest with meaningful, comfortable, and memorable travel experiences. Our aim is not merely to sell tour packages, but to create journeys that leave lasting impressions and heartfelt memories.

We are driven by a strong commitment to guest satisfaction, service quality, transparency, and ethical business practices. Every itinerary we plan reflects our attention to detail, personalized care, and dedication to safety and comfort.

Our goal is to present the true spirit of Uttarakhand—its spirituality, natural beauty, rich culture, and heritage—while ensuring a smooth, stress-free, and well-managed travel experience. Whether it is a pilgrimage, a leisure holiday, or a luxury Char Dham Yatra by helicopter, our focus always remains on quality, reliability, and trust.
