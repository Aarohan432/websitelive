Kedarnath Yatra from Chennai – Best 6 Days Plan

Plan a 6-day Kedarnath Yatra from Chennai, starting with a flight to Delhi and private transfers to Haridwar and Gaurikund via scenic Guptkashi. Enjoy comfortable hotel stays with breakfast and dinner, accompanied by a professional driver. Perfect for families and senior citizens.

  • Day 1: Fly from Chennai to Delhi, drive to Haridwar, overnight stay.
  • Day 2: Drive from Haridwar to Gaurikund, acclimatize and rest.
  • Day 3: Trek from Gaurikund to Kedarnath, visit Kedarnath temple, overnight stay.
  • Day 4: Trek back from Kedarnath to Gaurikund, relax.
  • Day 5: Drive from Gaurikund to Haridwar/Rishikesh, local sightseeing.
  • Day 6: Drive to Delhi, fly back to Chennai.

How Far is Kedarnath from Chennai?

Kedarnath is 2,602 km from Chennai, a 50-hour drive plus a 16 km trek. The fastest way is to fly to Dehradun (5 hours) and drive 245 km to Gaurikund, followed by the trek. By train, it takes 35-40 hours to Haridwar, then a 245 km drive to Gaurikund. Traveling from Chennai to Kedarnath typically involves a combination of air, rail, and road transportation, with the journey duration ranging from approximately 5 to 6 days.

What is the Route Map of Kedarnath from Chennai?

To travel from Chennai to Kedarnath: Fly to Dehradun (3-4 hours) or take a train to Haridwar (36-40 hours). From Rishikesh, take a taxi to Sonprayag (8-10 hours), then take a jeep to Gaurikund (30 minutes). Finally, trek (16-18 km), ride a pony, or take a helicopter to Kedarnath.

  1. Chennai to Dehradun or Haridwar (By Air or Train):
    • Depart from Chennai and travel to Dehradun’s Jolly Grant Airport or Dehradun/Haridwar railway station.
  2. Dehradun or Rishikesh to Guptkashi or Sonprayag (By Road):
    • From Dehradun or Rishikesh, proceed northwest towards Guptkashi or Sonprayag.
    • The road journey offers stunning views of the Himalayan landscape.
  3. Sonprayag to Kedarnath (By Trek or Pony Service):
    • Continue on foot or hire pony services for the trek to Kedarnath.
    • Stay overnight in Kedarnath
  4. Kedarnath to Sonprayag or Guptkashi (By Trek or Pony Service):
    • Trek back to Sonprayag and stay there for 1 night.
  5. Sonprayag or Guptkashi to Dehradun or Haridwar Drop (By Road):
    • Travel to Dehradun’s Jolly Grant Airport or Dehradun/Haridwar railway station and depart for Chennai.

What is the Best Way to Reach Kedarnath from Chennai?

The best way to reach Kedarnath from Chennai is by flight. Flying to New Delhi or Dehradun offers convenience and saves time, followed by a road journey to Sonprayag or Gaurikund.

Chennai to Kedarnath by flight:

  • The fastest way to reach Kedarnath from Chennai is by taking a flight to either Delhi or Dehradun.
  • From Chennai, multiple airlines operate flights to both Delhi and Dehradun. Popular airlines include IndiGo, SpiceJet, and Air India.

How Far is Kedarnath from Chennai by Flight?

From Chennai, fly to Dehradun or New Delhi, then travel by road or helicopter to Kedarnath. The flight from Chennai to Dehradun is about 1,800 to 2,000 kilometers (4 to 5 hours). The nearest airport to Kedarnath is Jolly Grant Airport in Dehradun, 220 kilometers from Gaurikund. Indira Gandhi International Airport in New Delhi is 450 kilometers from Gaurikund.

6 Days Chennai to Kedarnath Tour Itinerary

Day 01: Chennai to New Delhi (By Flight) to Haridwar (By Road)

  • Board a morning flight from Chennai to Delhi.
  • Arrive in Delhi and then proceed towards Haridwar.
  • Overnight stay in Haridwar.

Day 02: Haridwar to Gaurikund (230 Kms)

  • Next morning, drive towards Gaurikund.
  • Acclimatize to the altitude and weather conditions.
  • Rest and prepare for the trek.

Day 03: Gaurikund to Kedarnath (18 Kms Trek)

  • Start trekking from Gaurikund early in the morning.
  • Trek through lush green landscapes and alongside the Mandakini River.
  • Pass through Rambara and reach Kedarnath.
  • Visit the Kedarnath Temple upon arrival.
  • Overnight stay in Kedarnath.

Day 04: Kedarnath to Gaurikund(18 Kms Trek)

  • Descend back to Gaurikund.
  • Enjoy the scenic beauty along the way.
  • Reach Gaurikund by evening.
  • Rest and relax.

Day 05: Gaurikund to Haridwar/Rishikesh (230 Kms)

  • Drive towards Haridwar/Rishikesh.
  • Local sightseeing in Haridwar/Rishikesh.

Day 06: Haridwar/Rishikesh to New Delhi (By Road) to Chennai (By Flight)

  • Drive towards Delhi.
  • Board your flight to Chennai from New Delhi.
  • Conclude your journey with happy memories.

Note: The itinerary can be adjusted based on your pace, preferences, and the prevailing weather conditions. It’s crucial to stay updated on the weather forecast and any potential changes to the trekking route. Always follow safety guidelines and consult with local authorities or trek organizers.

Is There Any Direct Train from Chennai to Haridwar?

Currently, there is no direct trains between Chennai and Haridwar. One possible train route from Chennai to Haridwar includes a transit in Delhi, where travelers can switch trains to continue their journey to Haridwar.

Can we Travel to Kedarnath from Chennai Directly by Road?

Traveling directly to Kedarnath from Chennai by road is not feasible because of the considerable distance (2,400 Kilometers) between the two locations. Instead, travelers from Chennai usually opt for more convenient transportation options such as flights or trains to reach closer to Kedarnath, such as Delhi or Dehradun, and then proceed by road or trekking to the holy shrine.
