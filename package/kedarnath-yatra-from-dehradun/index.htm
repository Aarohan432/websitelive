Daywise Itinerary

Day 01: Dehradun to Guptkashi (210Kms / 07 to 08 Hrs)
Arrive in Dehradun and travel to Guptkashi, passing the Bhagirathi and Mandakini rivers en route. Check in at the hotel, followed by dinner and an overnight stay.

Day 02: Guptkashi – Kedarnath (6kms By Road & 16kms Trek One Way)
Journey to Kedarnath either by trekking or taking a helicopter (additional cost). Participate in Puja, Darshan, and the evening Aarti at Kedarnath.

Day 03: Kedarnath – Guptkashi (16kms Trek & 06kms By Road)
After Puja and Darshan at Kedarnath, explore the nearby areas. Return to Guptkashi by trek or helicopter, with dinner and an overnight stay.

Day 04: Guptkashi – Dehradun (210kms / 07 To 08hrs)
Travel back to Dehradun, with stops at Devprayag and Rishikesh along the way. The tour concludes upon arrival in Dehradun.

Package Includes

  • Daily breakfast & dinner at hotel
  • All sightseeing’s by Private car
  • Transfers: Destination-Hotel-Destination
  • Sight-seeing as per tour itinerary
  • All toll taxes, parking fees and driver’s allowance.

Package Excludes

  • Charges for early Check-In & late Check-Out at Hotel.
  • Rest all meals.
