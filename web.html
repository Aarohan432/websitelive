Uttarakhand Trips & Hospitality is your top-rated guide for scenic tours to Uttarakhand. Recognized by TripAdvisor and approved by UTDB, we’re passionate about sharing the beauty of this Himalayan gem.

Top Places In Uttarakhand

Uttarakhand is home to famous temples like Kedarnath and Badrinath, peaceful towns like Rishikesh and scenic hill stations like Mussoorie and Auli. From spiritual yatras to nature holidays, there are many places to visit.

Haridwar: Famous for the evening Ganga Aarti.

Rishikesh: The Yoga Capital of the world.

Kedarnath & Badrinath: Part of the holy Char Dham circuit.

Auli & Chopta: Best for snow and nature lovers.

Luxury Escapes In Uttarakhand

Places purely made for slowing down. From Taj Rishikesh to JW Marriott Mussoorie, enjoy mountain views and quiet mornings in comfort.
