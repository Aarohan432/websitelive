Health Advisory for Chardham Yatra by Helicopter

Chardham Yatra by helicopter includes visits to Yamunotri, Gangotri, Kedarnath, and Badrinath, all located between 8,500 and 11,500 feet above sea level. These are high-altitude regions where oxygen levels drop and air pressure is lower than normal. Sudden changes in altitude, especially with fast helicopter movement, can lead to altitude-related health issues.

Since the helicopter tour to Char Dham is completed in just 4 to 5 days, the body may not have time to adapt. Continuous travel, short layovers, and walking uphill without rest can trigger high altitude sickness.

Acute Mountain Sickness during Char Dham travel is common when you cross 2,500 meters in altitude. Symptoms include breathing difficulty, nausea, vomiting, headache, dizziness, fatigue, weakness, appetite loss, and sleep issues.

High Altitude Cerebral Edema during Kedarnath trip is a severe condition caused by brain swelling. This can begin with confusion, blurred vision, shortness of breath, coordination problems, and body control issues. If untreated, it can become fatal within hours.

Pulmonary edema in Badrinath or Kedarnath is another critical condition. It is caused by fluid buildup in the lungs. Warning signs include dry cough, breathlessness even at rest, chest tightness, red or pink sputum, drowsiness, and irregular heartbeat.

During your Char Dham helicopter journey, move slowly, rest after landing, eat light food, drink enough water, and avoid alcohol or smoking. Stay covered in warm layers and keep an eye out for any symptoms.

If you have heart conditions, lung problems, asthma, or any breathing disorder, consult your doctor before booking a Char Dham helicopter tour. Share your medical background with the tour operator. Hospitals are not available at high altitudes. Medical evacuation by helicopter is possible, but it depends on the weather and landing permissions.

This advisory is for travelers planning the Do Dham or Char Dham Yatra by air. Knowing the risks and responding early helps prevent health complications and ensures a safer pilgrimage experience.

Q. Is VIP darshan included in Char Dham helicopter packages?

A. VIP darshan ticket counter facility is available only in Kedarnath and Badrinath Dham. In Gangotri and Yamunotri Dham there are no special counters for VIP tickets however special pooja can be conducted through the temple’s priests.

A. No, The Helicopters land 6 kms away from Yamunotri temple, from there you will get pony/palkis to Yamunotri temple.
