Top Places In Uttarakhand

Uttarakhand is one of the top travel states in North India. It's home to famous temples like Kedarnath and Badrinath, peaceful towns like Rishikesh and Gangotri, and scenic hill stations like Mussoorie and Auli.

Rishikesh

Yoga Capital of the World, known for Ganga Aarti and white-water rafting.

Jim Corbett

India's first tiger reserve, perfect for wildlife safaris.

Mussoorie

Queen of Hills with beautiful views of the Doon Valley.

Kedarnath

One of the most important Shiva temples in India.

Haridwar: One of the holiest cities in India, known for evening Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri.

Badrinath: Dedicated to Lord Vishnu, located along the Alaknanda River.

Auli: Snow-covered mountain town known for skiing and panoramic Himalayan views.

Chopta: Mini Switzerland of Uttarakhand, base for Tungnath Temple trek.

Nainital: Beautiful lake town with Naini Lake at its center.
