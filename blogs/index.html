Sacred Destinations of Uttarakhand

Discover the divine beauty and spiritual significance of Dev Bhoomi

Kedarnath Dham

📍 Rudraprayag, Uttarakhand

One of the 12 Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva, located at 11,755 feet. A sacred pilgrimage site surrounded by snow-capped peaks.

Badrinath Dham

📍 Chamoli, Uttarakhand

Dedicated to Lord Vishnu, situated along the Alaknanda River. One of the most important Char Dham sites.

Om Parvat

📍 Vyas Valley, Uttarakhand

A natural wonder where snow forms the shape of 'Om' on the mountain peak, visible near the Tibet border.

Devprayag

📍 Tehri Garhwal, Uttarakhand

Sacred confluence of Alaknanda and Bhagirathi rivers, where the holy Ganga begins its journey.

Char Dham Yatra

Complete your spiritual journey with the sacred Char Dham circuit

Gangotri Dham

📍 Uttarkashi, Uttarakhand

Origin of the holy River Ganga. The temple is dedicated to Goddess Ganga and is one of the four Char Dham sites.

Yamunotri Dham

📍 Uttarkashi, Uttarakhand

Source of the Yamuna River and the starting point of the Char Dham Yatra. Features hot water springs and stunning mountain views.

Spiritual Towns

Experience peace, yoga, and divine energy on the banks of Ganga

Rishikesh

📍 Dehradun, Uttarakhand

Yoga capital of the world, famous for Ganga Aarti, adventure sports like rafting, and spiritual ashrams.

Haridwar

📍 Haridwar, Uttarakhand

Gateway to the Gods. Famous for Har Ki Pauri Ganga Aarti and starting point of Char Dham Yatra.

Beautiful Hill Stations

Escape to the serene mountains of Uttarakhand

Nainital

📍 Nainital, Uttarakhand

Lake district of Uttarakhand. Famous for Naini Lake, Mall Road, and scenic viewpoints like Snow View.

Mussoorie

📍 Dehradun, Uttarakhand

Queen of Hills. Known for Kempty Falls, Gun Hill, Camel's Back Road, and stunning Doon Valley views.

Adventure Destinations

For thrill-seekers and nature lovers

Auli

📍 Chamoli, Uttarakhand

India's premier skiing destination. Offers panoramic views of Nanda Devi and other Himalayan peaks.

Chopta

📍 Rudraprayag, Uttarakhand

Mini Switzerland of India. Base for Tungnath Temple trek and Chandrashila summit.

Travel Tips

Essential advice for your Uttarakhand trip

📅

Best Time to Visit

May-June and September-October are ideal for most destinations.

👕

Packing Essentials

Carry warm clothes, comfortable shoes, and rain gear.

🚗

Road Conditions

Mountain roads can be tricky. Hire experienced local drivers.

🏨

Accommodation

Book hotels in advance during peak season (May-June).
